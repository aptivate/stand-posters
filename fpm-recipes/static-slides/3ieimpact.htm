Using evidence to find out what works in development

3ie funds impact evaluations and systematic reviews that generate high quality evidence on what works in development and why. Evidence on development effectiveness can inform policy and improve the lives of poor people.

Search evidence database

3ie's Transparency and Accountability Thematic Window requests proposals

3ie invites proposals for impact evaluations of interventions that aim to increase the transparency and accountability in the governance of natural resources such as oil, gas and minerals. The interventions should actively involve civil society or be led by civil society organisations. Application deadline 23:59 GMT, 18 December 2013.

read more

Measuring results: International and South Asian experiences in impact evaluation

3ie in association with the Administrative Staff College of India hosted a conference on 23 October 2013 at New Delhi to bring together policymakers, donors and evaluation experts to discuss ways to increase commitment to evaluation and evidence-based policymaking in South Asia.

read more

3ie launches Registry of International Development Impact Evaluations (RIDIE)

RIDIE is a prospective registry of impact evaluations related to development in low- and middle-income countries. This registry aims to increase the transparency and quality of impact evaluation research before it begins.

read more

3ie Request for Qualifications : impact evaluation of removal of user fees

3ie is inviting applications for a policy window preparation grant to conduct an impact evaluation of the contribution of the removal of user fees on universal health coverage in eastern, central and southern Africa. Deadline extended to 14 November 2013.
